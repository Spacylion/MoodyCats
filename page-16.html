My dedication and acknowledgment
There is no greater gift than the power of words and the story they can tell. Stories can teach and transform a child's mind beyond our imagination. As I watched my wife Jessica Grant read this book, "The Big Bed" (written by Bunmi Laditan  illustrated by Tom Knight ) a very thoughtful and fitting gift I must say from my sister in law, Melynda Duquette. I witness my 4-year-old son appetite for a great book and story grow with delight as he followed each word trying to read along. As a Product Designer It was within this moment I was inspired to take this book and see if I can bring it to life within the medium I love, the web, to share with the world, to make it as playful and interactive as the story itself. I do hope you've enjoyed this story and experience as I tried to share the same joy my son has for this book with you. Like the saying goes...

A book is a gift you can open again and again,

Anfernee Grant
UX UI Product Designer
